Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I make a flight booking on bookMyticket? ▶

You can book a flight on bookMyticket in five easy steps: Visit the flight booking page, enter your departure and arrival destinations, select travel dates, choose from available flights based on your preferences, and click ‘Book Now’. Alternatively, use the MakeMyTrip app with the same simple steps for a convenient booking experience on the go.

Q: Can I avail domestic flight offers on bookMyticket? ▶

Yes, you can. When booking domestic flights, any active special offers will be displayed automatically. You can use filters to sort flights by price or other preferences to easily find budget-friendly options with discounts applied.

Q: How can I avail budget air tickets on bookMyticket? ▶

To get budget air tickets, select the ‘Price’ filter when flight options appear. This sorts flights from the lowest to highest fare, making it easy to pick the most affordable ticket for your travel dates.

Q: Why could I not avail the flight booking offers at the time of checkout? ▶

Flight prices and offers update in real-time based on availability and demand. Sometimes, by checkout, offers may expire or seats may sell out, causing prices to change. We recommend verifying the latest price before completing your booking.
