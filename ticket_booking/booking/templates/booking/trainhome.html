Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I book a train ticket on bookMyticket? ▶

Booking a train ticket on bookMyticket is easy: Visit the train ticket booking page, enter your departure and arrival stations, select travel dates, browse available trains and classes, choose your preferred seat and fare, then click ‘Book Now’ to complete your booking.

Q: Are there any train ticket offers available on bookMyticket? ▶

Yes, bookMyticket offers special discounts and deals on train tickets regularly. When searching for trains, any active offers will be automatically applied and visible. Use filters to sort by price or train type to quickly find the best deals.

Q: How can I find budget-friendly train tickets on bookMyticket? ▶

To find budget-friendly train tickets, apply the ‘Price’ filter on the search results page. This sorts trains from lowest to highest fare, helping you select the most affordable ticket for your journey.

Q: Why might I not be able to avail train ticket offers at checkout? ▶

Train ticket prices and offers update in real-time. Offers may expire or seats may sell out before checkout, causing price changes. We recommend verifying the final price just before completing your booking to ensure you get the best deal.
